# Awwwards Visual Direction

Date: 2026-07-15

## Direction

SUBEROS should move toward an editorial studio presentation:

- one dominant idea per section;
- fewer simultaneous boxes;
- larger negative space;
- small navigation/index cues;
- controlled display typography;
- media-led composition without third-party imagery.

## Translation into the repo

- `src/features/work/`: active-discipline desktop portfolio plus stacked mobile list.
- `src/features/process/`: full-width numbered rows instead of equal cards.
- `src/features/contact/`: editorial close with real contact details first and an honestly blocked form.

## Rules kept

- No copied imagery.
- No copied wording.
- No fabricated clients or metrics.
- No exact recreation of Cipher or Share Bien structure.
- Motion remains optional and reduced-motion aware.
