# Copy Audit And Content System

Date: 2026-07-16

## Editorial decisions kept

- The site continues to present SUBEROS as a creative studio combining photography, design, production and web.
- No public portfolio case studies are invented.
- No clients, results, awards, locations or metrics were added without evidence.

## Copy corrections validated in Sprint 14.5

- The immersive block now explains its purpose explicitly instead of reading as decorative spectacle.
- `Servicios` and `Trabajo` now connect through real interaction instead of parallel isolated claims.
- Mobile navigation copy remains short and operational:
  - direct summary;
  - direct CTA;
  - real email and phone.

## Content system rules still in force

- Public portfolio stays withheld until approved materials exist.
- Contact stays honestly blocked until server delivery is verified.
- Motion copy must always explain function, not just atmosphere.
- Every CTA must resolve to a real section, action or route.

## Remaining copy risk

The site is now directionally strong, but the next content sprint should still validate final legal owner data and any future public portfolio wording before launch is considered complete.
