# Asset Requirements And Art Direction

Date: 2026-07-16

## Current visual direction

- Charcoal-to-black base
- Warm white typography
- Syncopate as the public display family
- Editorial scale, not SaaS UI language
- Blue light accents only as controlled support, not as the primary brand color

## Assets already valid for runtime

- SUBEROS icon and favicon family in `public/branding/`
- Self-hosted Syncopate in `public/branding/fonts/syncopate/`
- Approved abstract sequence assets in `public/motion/lab/suberos-sequence-lab/`

## Assets still missing for a richer public portfolio

- Approved portfolio imagery per discipline
- Approved process stills or photography with publication clearance
- Social card variants tied to the final published launch state
- Optional future immersive sequence material with explicit publication approval

## Hard constraints

- No hotlinking from the legacy site
- No client imagery without publication approval
- No generated “fake portfolio” to fill space
- No third-party font hosting in production
